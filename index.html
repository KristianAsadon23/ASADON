HOW TO MAKE OATMEAL

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 Cup water or milk
  • Dash of salt (optional; for low sodium diets, omit salt)

Directions

  1. Boil water or milk and salt.
  2. Stir in oats.
  3. Cook about 5 minutes over medium heat; stir occasionally.
